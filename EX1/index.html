One Piece

One Piece is the name the world gave to all the treasure gained by the Pirate King Gol D. Roger. At least a portion of it once belonged to Joy Boy during the Void Century. The treasure is said to be of unimaginable value, and is currently located on Laugh Tale, the final island of the Grand Line. Here image

Conclusion

The conclusion of One Piece is that all the races and people of the world will emerge from a great flood together, entering a new dawn. This will happen when Joy Boy defeats the evil god who brought the flood, and a giant ark is pulled by sea kings across the world to save everyone.
